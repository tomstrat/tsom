Who are This Side of Midnight?

This Side of Midnight are a up and coming punk rock band based in Southampton UK.

Currently taking the south of England by storm with their fresh and explosive sound, TSOM want to inject new life into the rock scene and get live music back on the radar.

TSOM takes inspiration from bands like All Time Low, A Day To Remember & Foo Fighters. Their sound is familiar but has a strong new take on the genre.
